// manifesto / v4

A label, not a product.

{Brand} // Brooklyn, NY first written 2014 / last revised 2025-11-03

// seven rules, signed by all of us

  • Artists get paid first. 68% of every sale, before rent, before printing, before us.
  • 300 copies per run. Never more, never less. Scarcity is a byproduct, not a gimmick.
  • No exclusivity deals. Artists own their masters. They can walk tomorrow. We earn the next record.
  • No sponsored posts. Ever. Write us if you disagree.
  • No algorithm-led releases. We release what we like. We like what we release.
  • Zine on paper. Four issues a year, mailed on newsprint. The web version lags by a month on purpose.
  • Open ledger. 2025 books published every January 14, whether anyone reads them or not.
