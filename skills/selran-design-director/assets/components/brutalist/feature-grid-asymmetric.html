// section 02 / how we work

Five rules we don't break.

01

Artists get paid first

68% of every sale goes to the artist. We publish our ledger, line by line, every year. If you buy a $26 record, the artist sees $17.68 before we see a cent. We are a record label because someone had to do it right.

02

300 copies / run

Never more, never less. If it sells out, you wait for a repress. If it doesn't, we eat the loss.

03

No exclusivity deals

Our artists own their masters. They can leave tomorrow. Most of them stay. Most of them don't.

04

Zine on paper

22 issues so far. Mailed to subscribers on newsprint. Available at Printed Matter and the shows.

05

Shows you can find us at

86 since 2014. DIY halls, basements, back rooms. Tour schedule updated Thursdays.
