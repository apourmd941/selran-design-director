What we stand for

Nothing we sell arrives unannounced, unknown, or unrepairable.

Maker-first sourcing

Every piece is made by a named workshop we've visited — 34 of them, across 12 countries. The maker's card in your package tells you their story, their city, and how to write to them. We keep the margin honest and show it on our website.

Ten-year mender's promise

Broken handle? Cracked glaze? Send it back. We'll mend or replace — for ten whole years.

Honest materials only

No blends, no coatings we won't name. Linen, stoneware, oak, beeswax. Simple ingredients, listed plainly.

A store, not a warehouse

Two brick-and-mortar shops in Brooklyn and Lisbon. Free mending at the counter, always.

Seasonal, not endless

Four collections a year, rooted in the month. No dropshipped trend pieces, no flash sales.
