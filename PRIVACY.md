# Privacy Policy — Selran Design Director

Effective: 2026-06-10

Selran Design Director runs entirely on your machine, inside your own Claude
session.

- **No data collection.** The plugin does not collect, transmit, store, or
  share any user data, code, or telemetry. It has no backend, no analytics,
  and makes no network calls of its own.
- **Local artifacts only.** Everything Design Director produces — design
  systems (`design-system.md`), rendered artifacts, picker pages — is written
  to your local project and stays under your control.
- **Your Claude session.** Any code or content processed while using the
  skills is handled by your own Claude subscription under Anthropic's
  privacy terms; Design Director adds no additional processing or recipients.

One exception, fully opt-in: the Figma sync feature calls the Figma API
with a token **you** provide (`FIGMA_TOKEN`); nothing is sent anywhere unless
you configure and invoke it.
